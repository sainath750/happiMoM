# Bloom: Women's Health & Motherhood Platform Content

This repository contains all the content for Bloom, a modern women's health and motherhood care platform. The content is organized into separate files for easy use in website development, marketing materials, or app copy.

## Content Files

### `content.md`
Complete content document with all sections, features, and information in one place. Use this as a master reference.

### `hero-content.md`
Hero section content including headline, subtext, and primary call-to-action buttons. Perfect for landing pages and homepage headers.

### `features-content.md`
Detailed feature descriptions organized by category:
- Primary Features (Core System)
- Secondary Features (Support & Wellbeing)

Each feature includes a title, description, and bullet points.

### `trust-safety-content.md`
Trust, safety, and credibility content including:
- Privacy and security information
- Science-backed credentials
- Community support
- Emergency and safety guidelines

### `cta-buttons.md`
Collection of call-to-action button text options for different sections and contexts.

## Content Tone & Style

All content follows these guidelines:

- **Warm & Supportive**: Encouraging, empathetic language
- **Trustworthy & Scientific**: Evidence-based, medically informed
- **Friendly Companion**: Approachable, not clinical
- **Celebratory**: Acknowledges strength and beauty of womanhood
- **Reassuring**: Provides comfort during vulnerable moments
- **Empowering**: Helps women feel confident in health decisions

## Visual Design Notes

- **Color Palette**: Soft, warm tones (gentle pinks, calming blues, nurturing greens, warm creams)
- **Imagery**: Real, diverse women and families; natural, soft lighting
- **Typography**: Clean, readable, approachable yet professional
- **Icons**: Gentle, rounded, friendly
- **Overall Feel**: Warm hug, trusted friend, reliable medical resource

## Usage

These content files can be used for:
- Website copy (homepage, features pages, about page)
- App store descriptions
- Marketing materials
- Email campaigns
- Social media content
- In-app copy and UI text
